This woolen scarf provides the wearer with a +4 bonus on Fortitude saves made to resist the effects of cold weather (see Cold Dangers, page 302 of the Dungeon Master’s Guide).

 A scarf occupies the same space on the body as an amulet, necklace, or other item worn around the neck.

 Faint abjuration; 
CL 3rd; 
Craft Wondrous Item, endure elements; 
Price 250 gp.
