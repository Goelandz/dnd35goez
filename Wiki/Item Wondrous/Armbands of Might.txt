These bronze armbands grant their wearer a +2 bonus on Strength checks and Strengthbased skill checks. If the wearer has the Power Attack feat, he gains a +2 bonus on melee damage rolls on any attack on which he uses the Power Attack feat and takes a penalty of at least –2 on his attack roll.

 Armbands occupy the same space on the body as a pair of bracers or bracelets.

 Faint transmutation; 
CL 3rd; 
Craft Wondrous Item, bull’s strength; 
Price 4,100 gp.
