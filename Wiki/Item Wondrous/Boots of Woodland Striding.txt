These durable boots feel amazingly light when picked up. The boots allow full movement in forest or jungle terrain, including moderate or heavy obstruction (see Movement in Chapter 9 of the Player’s Handbook). In addition, the wearer can detect snares and pits at will and tree stride once per day, as the spells from a 12thlevel caster.

Caster Level: 12th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Wondrous Item, detect snares and pits, tree stride; 
Market Price: 23,600 gp; 
Weight: 1 lb.
