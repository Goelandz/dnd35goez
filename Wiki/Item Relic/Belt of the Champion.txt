Kord gives one of these gem-studded belts to a favorite gladiator each decade. The wearer gains a +6 bonus on Strength checks and an additional +4 bonus on grapple and bull-rush checks. If the wearer of a belt of the champion ever fails a save against a fear effect, the belt ceases functioning for 1 hour.

 To use this relic, you must worship Kord and either sacrifi ce a 5th-level divine spell slot or have the True Believer feat and at least 9 HD.
