These black leather boots leave no tracks wherever they go. They also render the wearer immune to the effects of fatigue and exhaustion as long as he ends each round at least 10 feet away from where he started it.

 It is said that once a century, Fharlanghn gives a pair of boots of the unending journey to a fellow traveler, and then magically endows an ordinary cobbler with the power to make him a new pair.

 To use this relic, you must worship Fharlanghn and either sacrifi ce a 6th-level divine spell slot or have the True Believer feat and at least 11 HD.

 Moderate conjuration; 
CL 11th; Sanctify Relic, Craft Wondrous Item, restoration, pass without trace, creator must worship Fharlanghn; 
Price 23,600 gp; 
Weight 1 lb.
