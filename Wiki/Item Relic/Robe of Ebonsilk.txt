Said to be woven from silk extruded by Lolth herself, a robe of ebonsilk gives the wearer a +8 armor bonus, a +4 resistance bonus on all saving throws, and continual spider climb and freedom of movement effects.

To use this relic, you must worship Lolth and either sacrifice an 8th-level divine spell slot or have the True Believer feat and at least 15 HD.
