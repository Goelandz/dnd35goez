The fortuitous creation of a poisoner with little skill, salvo is a weapon of the imprecise and desperate.

The poison is relatively cheap and easy to create, but its effect fluctuates wildly from dose to dose.

Salvo deals 1d4 points of initial and secondary damage to a random ability score. Determine the affected ability by rolling 1d6 and consulting the following table; roll once per dose. The poison’s type and save DC do not vary.

d6	Ability Damaged
1 	Strength
2 	Dexterity
3 	Constitution
4 	Intelligence
5 	Wisdom
6 	Charisma
