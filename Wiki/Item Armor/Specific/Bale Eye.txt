This suit of +2 half-plate is completely covered with engravings of sinister-looking eyes.  Three times per day, the owner can cast emotion (fear only) as an 11th-level sorcerer. The wearer also gains a +4 morale bonus on Will saves against fear effects.

Caster Level: 7th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Magic Arms and Armor, emotion, remove fear; 
Market Price: 38,360 gp;
Cost to Create: 19,805 gp + 1,504 XP
