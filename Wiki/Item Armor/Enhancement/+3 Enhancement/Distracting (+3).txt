Armor or shields with this quality shimmer and glow hypnotically. Anyone engaged in combat with the wearer must succeed on a Will save each round or be affected by the daze spell. Once per day, the wearer can activate hypnotic pattern (as the spell from a 9th-level caster).

Caster Level: 9th; 
Prerequisites: 
Craft Magic Arms and Armor, daze, hypnotic pattern; 
Market Price: +3 bonus.
