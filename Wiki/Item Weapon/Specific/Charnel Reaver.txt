This +3 dwarven waraxe, usually created by duergar smiths, utterly consumes the physical form of any living creature it slays, requiring a true resurrection spell to bring the creature back to life (raise dead and resurrection will not work).

Caster Level: 9th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Magic Arms and Armor, charnel fire; 
Market Price: 108,330 gp; 
Weight: 15 lb.
