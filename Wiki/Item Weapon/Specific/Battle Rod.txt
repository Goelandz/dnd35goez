This 3-foot adamantine rod acts as a +1 heavy mace. It provides a +2 morale bonus to saves vs. fear for all allies within 30 feet and allows the bearer to deliver messages to anyone within nine miles as the spell whispering wind.

Caster Level: 9th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Rod, whispering wind; 
Market Price: 35,712 gp; 
Weight: 8 lbs.
