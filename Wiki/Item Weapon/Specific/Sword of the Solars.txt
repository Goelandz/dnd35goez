This awesome weapon is a Large +5 dancing vorpal bastard sword. It always shines light equivalent to a torch (20-foot radius). This weapon’s high effective enhancement bonus makes it an epic weapon, beyond the reach of most player characters. The Epic Level Handbook has rules for creating such weapons.
