Through intentional starvation and macabre operations, the character is grossly underweight. He has a skeletal appearance, and his weight is now half normal for creatures of his kind. 

Prerequisite: Willing Deformity.

Benefit: The character gains a +2 deformity bonus to

Dexterity and a –2 deformity penalty to Constitution. Furthermore, he gains a +2 circumstance bonus on Escape
Artist checks and Intimidate checks.

Special: A character with this feat may not take the Deformity (obese) feat.
