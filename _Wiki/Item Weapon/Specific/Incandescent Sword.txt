This +4 holy greatsword glows with a bright light (a 30-foot radius equivalent to a sunrod) when it is held by a character of chaotic good alignment. It is the preferred weapon of ghaele eladrins.

Caster Level: 12th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Magic Arms and Armor, holy smite, light, creator must be chaotic good; 
Market Price: 72,400 gp; 
Cost to Create: 36,375 gp + 2,882 XP.
