This 3-foot iron rod allows the bearer to speak with animals at will when it is held. While mounted and bearing this item, the user gives the mount a +2 morale bonus on all saving throws. The goad of mastery also acts as a +1 heavy mace, but only while the bearer is mounted.

Caster Level: 5th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Magic Arms and Armor, speak with animals; 
Market Price: 20,980 gp;
Weight: 8 lb.
