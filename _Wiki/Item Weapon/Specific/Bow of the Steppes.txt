The magical properties imbued in this +2 mighty (+2) composite shortbow make it easier to use when mounted. Shots fired from the bow of the steppes while mounted incur only a –2 penalty on attacks if the wielder’s mount takes a double move. Shots fired while the wielder’s mount is running take only a –4 penalty on attacks.

Caster Level: 9th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Magic Arms and Armor, phantom steed; 
Market Price: 18,525 gp; 
Cost to Create: 9,525 gp + 720 XP
