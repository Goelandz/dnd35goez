This pear-shaped masterwork lute grants a +2 circumstance bonus on Perform (string instruments) checks and a +2 competence bonus on bardic music checks for countersong, fascinate, and suggestion. The cithern can be played by anyone with 4 ranks in Perform (string instruments) to cast cure light wounds, mage armor, and sleepeach once per day.
