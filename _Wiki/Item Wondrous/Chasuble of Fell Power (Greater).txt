This long, embroidered strip of scarlet cloth is covered with arcane sigils and mystic designs. Worn over the neck (occupying space on the body as an amulet), a chasuble of fell power adds 2d6 points of damage (greater) to any eldritch blast the wearer invokes, or to any spell the wearer casts that deals hit point damage and has the chaotic descriptor.

 Moderate evocation; 
9th (greater); 
Craft Wondrous Item, creator must be able to invoke an eldritch blast or cast chaos hammer; 
18,000 gp  (greater).
