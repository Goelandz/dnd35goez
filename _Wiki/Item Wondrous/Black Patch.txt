This black leather eyepatch conveys no extraordinary abilities except to those of orc blood. Any orc or half-orc who wears this patch over an empty eye socket or blind eye gains a +2 competence bonus on ranged attack rolls and on saving throws against illusions.

Caster Level: 6th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Wondrous Item;
Market Price: 8,800 gp;
