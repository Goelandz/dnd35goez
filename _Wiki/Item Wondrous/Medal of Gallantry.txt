This silver sun-shaped brooch grants its wearer a +2 bonus on Diplomacy checks but imposes a –2 penalty on Bluff checks. This penalty remains for 24 hours after the medal is removed.

 Three times per day the wearer can use sanctuary on himself as a swift action. If the wearer attacks while protected by this effect, he takes a –1 morale penalty on that attack and all subsequent attacks for 1 hour afterward.

 Faint abjuration; 
CL 3rd; 
Craft Wondrous Item, sanctuary; 
Price 1,100 gp.
