Worn by worshipers of the goddess of the woodlands, this emerald carving of a unicorn rampant sometimes seems to blend into the wearer's garb. When grasped and used as a focus for a spell, it exudes a faint green glow. 

When Ehlonna's brooch is used as a focus for a summon animal or summon monster spell, each creature summoned gains +1 hit point per Hit Die.
