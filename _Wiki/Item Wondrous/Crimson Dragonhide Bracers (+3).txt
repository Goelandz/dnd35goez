These bracers, crafted from the armored hide of a red dragon, grant their wearer an enhancement bonus of +3 to his natural armor. They also grant resistance to fire 5.

Faint to strong abujuration and transmutation; CL 9th (bracers +3);
Forge Ring, barkskin, resist energy, creator’s caster level must be three times that of the bonus placed in the bracers;
Price 21,000 gp (bracers +3);
Weight 1 lb.
