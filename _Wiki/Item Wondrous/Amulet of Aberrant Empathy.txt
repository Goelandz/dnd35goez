This strange, twisted amulet allows the wearer to use the Handle Animal skill in place of the Diplomacy skill when dealing with some aberrations.

 While wearing an amulet of aberrant empathy, a character can make Handle Animal checks rather than Diplomacy checks to influence the attitude of aberrations with an Intelligence of 9 or lower.

Moderate enchantment; 
CL 11th; 
Craft Wondrous Item, charm monster;
Price 1,400 gp.
