These ioun stones (usually burnt orange in color) possess all the features of a standard ioun stone. Ioun stones of resistance offer magic protection in the form of a +4 resistance bonus on all saving throws.

Caster Level: 5th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Wondrous Item, resistance, caster level must be three times that of the ioun stone’s bonus; 
Market Price: 32,000 gp (+4); 
Weight: —.
