This metal disk has straps that go over a dragon’s forelimbs to hold the pectoral in place over its chest. A winged creature wearing a pectoral of maneuverability has its flying maneuverability improved by one step. A humanoid can wear a pectoral as if it were a vest.

Faint (ordinary) transmutation; 
CL 3rd (ordinary); 
Craft Wondrous Item, wings of air (ordinary); 
Price 12,000 gp (ordinary); 
Weight 5 lb.
