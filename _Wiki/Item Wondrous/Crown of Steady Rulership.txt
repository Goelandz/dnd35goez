This ornate golden crown is a useful tool for princes and potentates, for it allows them to see through the falsehoods that parade before them. The wearer gains a +5 competence bonus on Sense Motive checks and on Spot checks made to see through disguises.

 Faint divination; 
CL 3rd; 
Craft Wondrous Item, see invisibility; 
Price 4,500 gp.
