These spectacles grant the wearer a +5 competence bonus on Spellcraft checks made to decipher scrolls, and a +5 competence bonus on Use Magic Device checks made to use scrolls.

 Spectacles occupy the same space on the body as a pair of eye lenses or goggles.

 Faint divination; 
CL 3rd; 
Craft Wondrous Item, read magic; 
Price 2,500 gp.
