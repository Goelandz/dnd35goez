This 6-inch Vasharan orb appears to be made from teeth and bones. When held above a rotting, maggot-infested corpse for 1 round, it draws the maggots into itself. The maggots can then be used to allow the wielder to speak and understand the Dark Speech (see Chapter 2) for 1 minute.

Caster Level: 8th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Wondrous Item, tongues, creator must be evil; Market Price: 10,000 gp; Weight:
5 lb
