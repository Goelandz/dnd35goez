The wearer of these goggles gains a +10 bonus on Spot checks and enjoys low-light vision and 60-foot darkvision. Once per day, the wearer can command the goggles to grant him blindsense for 1 minute.

The goggles also protect the wearer from being blinded by the cloud created by a hovering dragon (though the cloud still provides concealment for all within it).

Moderate transmutation; 
CL 9th; 
Craft Wondrous Item, dragonsight; 
Price 46, 000 gp.
