This golden brooch provides its wearer with a +2 morale bonus on saves against fear effects. This bonus improves to +4 on level checks made to avoid being intimidated.

 The morale bonus of this badge stacks with the morale bonus granted by the paladin’s aura of courage ability and the bard’s inspire courage ability.

Faint enchantment; 
CL 4th; 
Craft Wondrous Item; heroism; 
Price 1,000 gp.
