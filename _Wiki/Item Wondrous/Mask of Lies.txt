This black, featureless mask only has slits for the eyes and mouth. The wearer can cast change self on himself at will and is continually under the effect of undetectable alignment. In addition, he or she gains a +5 bonus on Bluff checks.

Caster Level: 5th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Wondrous Item, change self, undetectable alignment; 
Market Price: 17,000 gp; 
Weight: —.
