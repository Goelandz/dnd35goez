This tiny vial contains a murky, pungent liquid. When consumed, nature’s draught cause subtle changes in the user’s scent. Animals respond well to a character who has consumed nature’s draught, finding her less threatening and easier to trust. Drinking a vial of nature’s draught provides a +1 circumstance bonus on Handle Animal and Animal Empathy checks made during the next day.
