These saddlebags open into an extradimensional space, allowing them to hold much more than they would seem able to. Saddlebags of holding always come in pairs. Each bag contains 30 cubic feet of space and can hold 250 pounds. They otherwise operate identically to the bag of holding 1 described in the DUNGEON MASTER’S Guide.

Prerequisites: Craft Wondrous Item, Leomund’s secret chest;
