This outfit consists of loose, billowy clothing designed to keep the wearer cool and protected from the sun while out in dry, hot, desert terrain. It includes a caftan, turban, scarf, loose pantaloons, and either high boots or low sandals. Wearing the outfit eliminates the –4 penalty on Fortitude saves for wearing heavy clothes in hot or extreme heat (see Heat Dangers in Chapter 3 of the DUNGEON MASTER’S Guide). This does not apply if the character wears any armor.
