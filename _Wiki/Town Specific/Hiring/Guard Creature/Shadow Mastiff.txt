With their bay ability, shadow mastiffs are best employed as personal escorts of evil masters or left among other evil creatures. Their natural abilities are also heightened underground, deep indoors, or just in the proximity of tall things that cast a lot of shadows, where light is less likely to hamper them.

	DC Young/Adult: 22/29; 
	Young Price: 10,000 gp; Training Cost: 3,000 gp.
