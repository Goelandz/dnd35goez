Alignment: Lawful Neutral

The deity of strategy and skill in warfare, Halmyr is patient and thoughtful in all things. He looks kindly upon those who use planning and forethought in their endeavors. Many generals and warlords offer up a prayer to Halmyr on the evening before a battle. Lawful characters of many classes, particularly fighters, monks, rogues, and even some wizards revere him. Halmyr’s clerics pray for spells at dusk.

Portfolio: Strategy, Skill in warfare

Favored Weapon: Rapier
