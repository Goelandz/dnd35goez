Olidammara 
The god of rogues, Olidammara, is chaotic neutral. His title is the Laughing Rogue. Olidammara delights in wine, women, and song. He is a vagabond, a prankster, and a master of disguise. His temples are few, but many people are willing to raise a glass in his honor. Rogues and bards are frequently among his worshipers. 

The domains he is associated with are Chaos, Luck, and Trickery. 

The rapier is his favored weapon.
