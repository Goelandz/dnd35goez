Erythnul 
The god of slaughter, Erythnul, is chaotic evil. His title is the Many. Erythnul delights in panic and slaughter. In civilized lands, his followers (including evil fighters, barbarians, and rogues) form small, criminal cults. In savage lands, 
evil barbarians, gnolls, bugbears, ogres, and trolls commonly worship him. 

The domains he is associated with are Chaos, Evil, Trickery, and War. 

His favored weapon is a morningstar with a blunt stone head
