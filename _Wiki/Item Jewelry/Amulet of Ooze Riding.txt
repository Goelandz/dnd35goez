Amulet of Ooze Riding 
Any time the wearer of this amulet comes into contact with an ooze, a sphere of force springs up around the wearer. The sphere prevents any contact with an ooze and protects the wearer from direct attacks by oozes.

Caster Level: 7th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Wondrous Item, Otiluke’s resilient sphere; 
Market Price: 28,000 gp
