Appearing to be little more than a rustic walking-stick, this staff allows use of the following spells:
• Remove fear (1 charge)
• Remove paralysis (1 charge)
• Remove curse (1 charge)
• Freedom of movement (2 charges)
• Break enchantment (2 charges)

Caster Level: 9th; 
Prerequisites: Craft Staff, break enchantment, freedom of movement, remove curse, remove fear, remove paralysis; 
Market Price: 45,000 gp.
