Forged from a silver-white mithral alloy, a mithralmist shirt is a +2 mithral shirt that fi lls the wearer’s square with a billowing silver mist on command. The mist grants the wearer concealment but does not interfere with his vision. The armor sheds silver mist for 1 minute per use, up to seven times per day. 

Finally, once per day, the wearer of this armor can assume gaseous form for up to 10 minutes.
